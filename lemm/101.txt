 universiade village was built for the 27th summer universiade 
 2013   currently 9000 kfu students live there – all the first year students and international 
 students   from over 80 countries   are among them   universiade village is one of the major 
 student campuses in russia uniting 20 modern five storey buildings and high rise apartment 
 blocks where kfu students can comfortably lodge   sports grounds   ice hockey rink   hobby 
 clubs are located on the territory of the universiade village for student leisure 
 activities 
 over 30 kazan university sports clubs on 27 different kinds of 
 sports such as futsal and basketball   volleyball and handball   track and field athletics 
 weightlifting   arm wrestling   sambo and judo   boxing and others welcome all the willing 
 student sport club jointly with the university department of physical education and sports 
 and kfu youth affairs department are in charge of the students ’ sports potential 
 development 
 those preferring outdoor leisure activities will definitely appreciate our clubs specializing 
 on climbing   sports orienteering   tourism   alpinism and speleology   the lovers of tennis 
 ping pong   badminton   cross country skiing   swimming and chess will also be treated to a 
 whole variety of activities 
 kfu student club is responsible for bringing creative young 
 people together and boosting their talents   over 90 art clubs specializing on different 
 fields carry out their activities and develop at kfu 
 kazan university is proud of its creative students and groups who are the champions and 
 prize winners of numerous contests of municipal   republican   regional   national and 
 international levels
